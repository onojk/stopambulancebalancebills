Stories from Patients Hit by Surprise Ambulance Bills

This page will collect anonymized stories from people across the United States who have been hit with large, unexpected ambulance bills — often after doing nothing more than calling 911 in an emergency.

Why stories matter

Policy debates can get abstract. Stories make the issue real: how a single bill can damage savings, delay care, or push families into debt — just because the wrong ambulance showed up.

Planned structure

  • Short, anonymized case summaries by state
  • Bill amounts, insurance coverage, and what patients were told
  • Outcomes: payment plans, collections, appeals, or negotiated reductions
