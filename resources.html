üìÑ Step-by-Step Guide: What to Do If You Get an Ambulance Balance Bill

Use this simple process to dispute a bill, understand your protections, and take the right next steps.

  1. Request an itemized bill from the ambulance provider.
  2. Check for coding errors or charges for services you did not receive.
  3. Ask your insurer why it paid the amount it did.
  4. Submit a written appeal to your insurer and provider (templates below).
  5. File a state complaint if the bill is unfair or predatory.
  6. Escalate to your state legislators and demand ambulance billing reform.
