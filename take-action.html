Take Action

Stopping ambulance balance billing will take voices from across the country. You can make a real impact — even with small actions.

1. Share your story

If you or someone you know has received a surprise ambulance bill, your experience matters. These stories help illustrate how widespread and harmful the problem is.

Send your experience →

2. Contribute research or insights

Policy researchers, health economists, EMTs, medical billing experts, and data analysts are invited to collaborate.

Start a collaboration →

3. Spread awareness

Share facts, link to the site, and tell people the truth: Ground ambulance billing is the last major loophole in U.S. emergency care.

4. Contact your representatives

Encourage lawmakers to support a national ban on ambulance balance billing. Ground ambulances must be included under federal surprise billing protections.
